Our History:


Roots & Growth
Founded in 2015, QMGL Real Estate began as a small friends-run agency in the heart of the Amman city. With a vision to simplify the home buying process, our founders, Qusai, Ghalia, Liyan and Mohanad, combined their passion for real estate with innovative technology to create a unique customer experience. Milestones & Achievements

2016: Launched our first website, providing virtual tours and online consultations.
2018: Expanded our services to include property management and commercial real estate.
2021: Celebrated a decade of service with over 10,000 families happily settled in their new homes.
2023:Introduced an AI-powered property matching system, revolutionizing how clients find their perfect home.
2024: Awarded “Best Real Estate Agency” for outstanding customer service and community involvement.

Today & Tomorrow
Today, QMGL Real Estate is a leading name in the real estate industry, with a network of over 50 branches nationwide. We remain committed to our core values of integrity, innovation, and excellence. As we look to the future, we are excited to continue growing, learning, and setting new benchmarks in the real estate market.
